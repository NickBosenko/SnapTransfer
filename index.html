Welcome to SnapTransfer

Need to transfer files between your phone and PC? It's easy!
From PC to phone — just as simple!

1. Connect your phone and PC to the same Wi-Fi network

2. Open Chrome or Safari on your phone

3. Go to: http://

All files are saved in the local Uploads folder, organized by the date they were uploaded.

If your browser shows a security warning, click "Advanced" → "Proceed anyway".

All your files are stored locally on your computer and are never uploaded to the internet!
